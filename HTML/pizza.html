Pizza

Pizza Recipe

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice:
    • Pepperoni
    • Sliced bell peppers
    • Sliced onions
    • Sliced mushrooms
    • Olives

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to preheat as well.
  2. If you're using store-bought pizza dough, follow the instructions on the package to roll it out. If you're making homemade dough, roll it out on a lightly floured surface into a round shape, about 12 inches in diameter.
  3. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza peel or a lightly floured surface. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your desired toppings evenly over the cheese. You can get creative here and add whatever toppings you like!
  6. If you're using a pizza stone, carefully transfer the pizza onto the hot stone in the oven. If not, you can bake the pizza on a baking sheet. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  7. Once the pizza is done, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade pizza!
