Adobo Recipe

  • 2 lbs chicken thighs or pork belly, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, marinate chicken or pork with soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, bay leaf, and peppercorns for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat cooking oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add marinated chicken or pork and cook until browned.
  3. Add water to the skillet, then cover and simmer for 30-40 minutes or until meat is tender.
  4. Remove the lid and continue to simmer until the sauce thickens.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice.
