Mac and Cheese Recipe

  • 2 cups macaroni pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bread crumbs (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the macaroni pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly, to make a roux.
  4. Gradually whisk in the milk until smooth. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens.
  5. Stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted and smooth.
  6. Season the cheese sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir until well combined.
  8. If desired, sprinkle bread crumbs over the top for added crunch.
  9. Transfer the mac and cheese to a baking dish and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20-25 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown on top.
  10. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly before serving.
