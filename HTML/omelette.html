Omelette Recipe

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your choice)
  • 1/4 cup diced vegetables (bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, mushrooms, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs and milk together until well combined.
  2. Stir in the shredded cheese and diced vegetables. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes or until the edges start to set.
  5. Using a spatula, gently lift the edges of the omelette and tilt the skillet to let the uncooked eggs flow to the edges.
  6. Continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes or until the omelette is set but still slightly moist on top.
  7. Fold the omelette in half with the spatula and slide it onto a plate. Serve hot.
