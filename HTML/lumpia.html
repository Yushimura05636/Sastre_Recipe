Lumpia Recipe

  • 1 pack lumpia wrappers
  • 1/2 pound ground pork or beef
  • 1/4 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped cabbage
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant.
  2. Add the ground pork or beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add the chopped carrots, green onions, and cabbage. Cook until the vegetables are softened.
  4. Season with soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well and cook for another minute.
  5. Remove from heat and let the filling cool slightly.
  6. Place a spoonful of the filling onto a lumpia wrapper. Fold the sides over the filling and roll tightly.
  7. Brush the edges of the wrapper with water to seal the lumpia.
  8. Heat oil in a pan for frying. Fry the lumpia until golden brown and crispy.
  9. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels. Serve hot with dipping sauce.
